## What is this?

A free bulk certificate generator that works entirely 
in your browser.

No data uploaded to any server.
No account needed.
No limit on certificates.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Event organizers waste hours manually editing 
certificate templates one by one in Canva or Photoshop.

150 participants = 3 hours of copy-paste work.

This tool does it in 30 seconds.

---

## How It Works

1. **Design your certificate** in Canva, Photoshop, 
   or anywhere — export as image (PNG/JPG)

2. **Make a CSV file** with participant names 
   (even Excel works — just save as CSV)

3. **Upload both** to the tool

4. **Drag the name text** to position it exactly 
   where you want on the certificate

5. **Click "Bulk Generate ZIP"**

6. **Download ZIP** — every certificate ready as 
   high-resolution PNG
